Clueless: Instructions

Objective

The goal of Clue is to solve the mystery of Mr. Boddy's murder. You need to determine:

  • Who committed the crime
  • Where the crime took place
  • What Weapon was used

Gameplay

  • Moving: Click on a location to move around the mansion. You can also use Secret Passages in the corner rooms.
  • Suggestions: When you enter a room, make a suggestion by naming a Suspect, Weapon, and the Room you're in. Other players try to prove your suggestion false by showing you one of the cards you named.
  • Accusations: When you think you know the solution, make an accusation by naming a Suspect, Weapon, and Room. You can only make one accusation during the game.

Winning

The player who correctly accuses the murderer, the room, and the weapon wins the game!
